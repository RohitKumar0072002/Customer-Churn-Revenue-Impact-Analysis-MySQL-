## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior using structured SQL queries across multiple business dimensions such as:

Contract Type
Tenure
City
Feature Adoption (Tech Support)
Revenue Impact
The objective is to identify key churn drivers and quantify the financial impact of customer attrition.

## Database Tables Used
customers
subscriptions
billing
services
churn
Relational joins were used to perform segmentation-based churn analysis.

## Key Business Metrics Calculated
Total Customers
Total Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
Overall Churn Rate
Revenue Lost Due to Churn
