## Telco Customer Churn & Revenue Impact

## Overall Business Health
Total Customers: 7,043
Total MRR: ₹455K
Churn Rate: ~26%
Revenue Lost: ₹139K
## Insight:
More than one-fourth of customers have churned, causing significant monthly revenue leakage.
## Contract Type Analysis
Month-to-Month contracts show ~42% churn.
Long-term contracts show below 11% churn.
## Business Meaning:
Short-term commitments are high-risk segments.
Annual plan conversion campaigns could significantly improve retention.
## Tenure Analysis
Nearly 50% churn occurs within the first year.
## Business Meaning:
Customer onboarding and early engagement are critical.
First-year retention strategies can reduce churn drastically.
## City-Based Risk Analysis
San Diego shows both high customer base and high churn (~33%).
## Business Meaning:
Certain geographic regions represent concentrated revenue risk.
Localized retention strategies may be required.
## Feature Adoption (Tech Support)
Customers without tech support churn at nearly 3x higher rate.
## Business Meaning:
Service adoption directly impacts retention.
Bundled tech support plans can act as churn prevention levers.

## Final Strategic Recommendation
The primary churn drivers identified are:
Month-to-month contracts
Early-stage customers
Lack of tech support
Suggested Actions:
Incentivize long-term contracts
Improve first-year onboarding
Promote tech support adoption
Run targeted retention campaigns in high-risk cities
